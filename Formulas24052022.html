ANGLE

  1. Acute angle: An angle < 90°
  2. Right angle: An angle = 90°
  3. Obtuse angle: An angle > 90° and < 180°
  4. Straight angle: An angle = 180°
  5. Reflex angle: An angle > 180° and < 360°
  6. Full angle: An angle = 360°

TRIANGLE

  1. Acute triangle: Each angle < 90°
  2. Right triangle: One angle = 90°
  3. Obtuse triangle: One angle > 90°
  4. Equilateral triangle: Length of all sides are equal
  5. Isosceles triangle: Length of 2 sides are equal
  6. Scalene triangle: Length of all sides are different
