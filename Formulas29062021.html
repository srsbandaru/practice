ANGLE

Acute angle: An angle < 90°

Right angle: An angle = 90°

Obtuse angle: An angle > 90° and < 180°

Straight angle: An angle = 180°

Reflex angle: An angle > 180° and < 360°

Full angle: An angle = 360°

TRIANGLE

Acute triangle: Each angle < 90°

Right triangle: One angle = 90°

Obtuse triangle: One angle > 90°

Equilateral triangle: Length of all sides are equal

Isosceles triangle: Length of 2 sides are equal

Scalene triangle: Length of all sides are different
